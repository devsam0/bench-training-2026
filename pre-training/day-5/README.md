What was the most surprising finding?
The most surprising finding was the difference in survival between classes. 62.9% of 1st-class passengers survived, only 24.2% of 3rd-class passengers survived. Even within the 3rd class, gender was the deciding factor: 50% of 3rd-class women survived compared to only 13.5% of 3rd-class men. It shows that "Women and Children First" was a real priority, but "Wealthy First" was the underlying reality.
